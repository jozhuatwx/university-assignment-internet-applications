Our Mission

Our mission is to guide and motivate all the badminton players, provide entertainment through exciting activities give impetus on fan experience and create innovative, influential sustainability initiatives.

Our Vision

To make badminton a global leader of sport that everyone can participate in. Provide every student with an opportunity to know more about Badminton.

Our Goals

Strengthen the links between members in club, prevent any disputes among members.

To let all the A.P.U student got change to enjoy the badminton sport.

Promote a badminton activity or competition in every certain period to prepare for future, join the world-class badminton events.
