APU-Sunway Tournament 2018

Dear APU students,

We are glad to announce that after months of planning, we will be having our annual In-House Tournament 2018 !!!!

This year, we are collaborating with Sunway University Badminton Club to make this event an even bigger one. So, if you don't have a partner in APU, you can ask your Sunway friends to join this competition with you!!! (Only APU and Sunway Students). Not only that, to make it even more special, we will be introducing a brand new category, which is the Touch and Go. Unlike the doubles event, instead there will be 4 players in a team. However, ex/current state and above players are not allowed to participate.

And to every participant, just top up an additional RM15 to grab our exclusive event t-shirt specially for this year's tournament. For non-participants, you can get it for only RM20.

The registration will begin on 11th June 2018 - 14th July 2018 at the APU Atrium. So what are you waiting for??? Visit our booth and SIGN UP!!!! Contact details are in the poster below.

Best regards,
Hoh Kok Wai
President of APU Badminton Club
