Equipment

Shuttlecock

  • A standard shuttlecock should have 16 feathers fixed in the cock.
  • The feathers must have a length between 62mm to 70mm when measured from the end of cock. Diameter of circle form by the feathers shall have 58mm to 68mm.
  • The cock shall have a diameter between 25mm to 28mm.
  • The whole shuttlecock weight should have around 4.74 to 5.50 grams.

Racket

  • A standard badminton racket shall not exceed 68cm length and 23cm wide at the overall part of the racket.
  • Badminton racket has divide into four-part, head, throat, shaft and handle:
  • Head part of badminton racket is to hit the shuttlecock and transfer the ball to the opposite player.
  • Throat and shaft is the body of a badminton racket to hold on the head and handle and make racket more stabilize while playing.
  • Handle of the racket is for the player to grab and function the racket.
